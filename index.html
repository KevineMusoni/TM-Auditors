About US


To become the leading Auditing and consulting firm in the region that provides excellent services to private and public institutions.



To provide high quality services in the areas of Finance and audit, Human Resource and Training, Research and Planning and General Consulting to private and public institutions in order to improve their performance and competitiveness.



TM Auditors and Consulting LTD, TM Auditors, in short, is a firm of specialized Auditors and Consultants of career in Finance and audit, Human Resource and Training, Research and Planning and General Consulting. It is registered under No 2195/KIG. The partners respectively worked in big cabinets of international reputation (PriceWater HouseCoopers, Deloitte and Touche, Stratégies 2000, Grant Thornton) and have confirmed experience in management of big institutions, reinforced by constant qualifications in accounting, finance, management, economics and auditing.

“TM Auditors” works with public and private enterprises, international organisations, non-governmental organisations, public service, financial institutions, banks, insurance companies, particular investors, different intervention projects, various economic operators.
